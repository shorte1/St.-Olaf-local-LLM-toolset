Prompt: Write a haiku about autumn, then translate it to French, then explain the seasonal symbolism used.
========================================
**Autumn Haiku**

Golden leaves descend,  
Crisp breeze whispers through tall trees,  
Earth wraps in gold hue.

**Translation into French:**

Feuilles d'or tombent lentement,  
Le vent frais murmure dans les arbres,  
La terre s'habille de doré.

**Seasonal Symbolism Explanation:**

The haiku captures the essence of autumn through vivid imagery. The "golden leaves descending" symbolizes the season's transition, as leaves change color and fall from trees. The "crisp breeze" represents the cooling weather that marks autumn's arrival. Finally, the earth "wrapping in gold hue" highlights the vibrant colors of autumn foliage, reflecting nature's transformation during this time of year. Each element evokes a sense of change and beauty characteristic of autumn.
