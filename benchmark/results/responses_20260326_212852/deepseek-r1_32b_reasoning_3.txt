Prompt: A lily pad doubles in size every day. It takes 48 days to cover a lake. How long does it take to cover half the lake? Show your working.
========================================
To determine how long it takes for the lily pad to cover half the lake, let's analyze its growth pattern step by step.

1. **Understanding the Growth:**
   - The lily pad doubles in size every day.
   - On day 48, the lily pad completely covers the lake.

2. **Reverse Calculation:**
   - Since the lily pad doubles each day, the size on any given day is half of what it will be the next day.
   
3. **Finding Half Coverage:**
   - If the lake is fully covered on day 48, then one day before (on day 47), the lily pad would have been half the size needed to cover the entire lake.

\[
\boxed{47}
\]

**Answer:** It takes \(\boxed{47}\) days for the lily pad to cover half of the lake.
