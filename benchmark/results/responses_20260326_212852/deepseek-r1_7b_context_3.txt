1) **Main Argument**:  
The main argument of the paragraph is that remote work is more productive than office work because people the speaker knows prefer working from home. - Empirical data on employee retention and turnover rates in companies that allow remote work versus those that do not.  
- Examples of well-known companies (e.g., Salesforce, Zoom) where employees have thrived in a remote work environment.
