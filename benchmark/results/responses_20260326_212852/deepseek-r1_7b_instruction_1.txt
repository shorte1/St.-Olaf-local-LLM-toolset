Prompt: Write a haiku about autumn, then translate it to French, then explain the seasonal symbolism used.
========================================
**Haiku:**

Autumn's touch softens the trees,
Leaves fall, a gentle breeze.

**French Translation:**

La impresión de otoño
menos cruel que la primavera.
Las hojas se caen con suave carcajuelo.

---

**Explanation of Seasonal Symbolism:**

In this haiku, autumn is depicted as a season that softens the trees and gently causes leaves to fall. The imagery suggests a transition from summer's vibrancy to the cooler, more reflective aspects of autumn. The "ojo de viento" (eye of the wind) symbolizes the subtle change in wind patterns that mark the end of summer and the start of winter. This season embodies transformation and the natural cycle of decay and preparation for renewal, capturing both the beauty and the transition that autumn brings.
