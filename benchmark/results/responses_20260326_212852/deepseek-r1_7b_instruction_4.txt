### **Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe**

#### **Ingredients**
- 2 cups (150 g) all-purpose flour
- 3/4 cup (90 g) granulated sugar
- 1/2 cup (60 g) brown sugar, packed
- 1 cup (8 oz) unsalted butter, softened
- 1 large egg
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2 cups (150 g) chocolate chips

#### **Instructions**

1. Preheat your oven to **375°F (190°C)**.
2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, granulated sugar, brown sugar, baking soda, and salt until well combined.
3. In another bowl, cream together the unsalted butter, egg, and vanilla extract until smooth.
4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture until just combined. Do not overmix.
5. Stir in the chocolate chips by eye until evenly distributed.
6. Roll the dough into 1-inch balls (smaller is better for larger cookies).
7. Place the cookie dough about **1 inch apart** on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
8. Flatten each dough ball with a rolling pin to about 1/2 inch thick.
9. Spoon or roll chocolate chips over the top of each flattened dough.
10. Bake in the preheated oven for **9-11 minutes**, until golden brown on top.

11. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

---

**Notes:**  
- Adjust the baking time if your cookies are smaller or larger than the standard size.  
- For soft and chewy cookies, use **unsalted butter**; for crispy ones, consider using **salted butter** or a shortening.  

Enjoy your homemade chocolate chip cookies!
