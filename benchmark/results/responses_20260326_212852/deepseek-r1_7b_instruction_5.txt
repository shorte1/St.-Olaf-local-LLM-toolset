### Formal Email Decline:

---

Dear [Hiring Manager/Recruiter],

I hope this message finds you well. Thank you for extending the offer to join my team at [Company Name]. I am deeply grateful for your trust and the opportunity to contribute to such an esteemed organization.

After careful consideration, I regret to inform you that I have declined the position. While I was genuinely interested in the role and its alignment with my career aspirations, I now realize that other commitments require my full attention at this time.

Please understand that this decision was not made lightly, as I held the offer in high regard. I trust that you will find an ideal candidate to fill the position, and I look forward to hearing from you.

Thank you once again for your understanding and consideration.

Warm regards,

[Your Full Name]  
[Your Job Title]  
[Company Name]  
[Contact Information]
