Prompt: If it takes 5 machines 5 minutes to make 5 widgets, how long would it take 100 machines to make 100 widgets? Show your working.
========================================
To determine how long it would take 100 machines to make 100 widgets, let's break down the problem step by step.

### Step 1: Determine the Production Rate of One Machine
- **Given:** 
  - 5 machines can produce 5 widgets in 5 minutes.
  
- **Calculation:**
  \[
  \text{Production rate per machine} = \frac{\text{Number of widgets}}{\text{Number of machines} \times \text{Time}} = \frac{5 \text{ widgets}}{5 \text{ machines} \times 5 \text{ minutes}} = \frac{1}{5} \text{ widget per machine per minute}
  \]
  
This means each machine can produce \( \frac{1}{5} \) of a widget every minute.

### Step 2: Calculate the Time to Produce 100 Widgets with 100 Machines
- **Given:** 
  - We have 100 machines and need to produce 100 widgets.
  
- **Production Rate for All Machines Together:**
  \[
  \text{Total production rate} = 100 \text{ machines} \times \frac{1}{5} \text{ widget per machine per minute} = 20 \text{ widgets per minute}
  \]
  
- **Time Required:**
  \[
  \text{Time} = \frac{\text{Total number of widgets}}{\text{Total production rate}} = \frac{100 \text{ widgets}}{20 \text{ widgets per minute}} = 5 \text{ minutes}
  \]
  
### Conclusion
It would take **\boxed{5 \text{ minutes}}** for 100 machines to produce 100 widgets.
