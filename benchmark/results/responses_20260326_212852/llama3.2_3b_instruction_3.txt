========================================
In Verona, two families' feud escalates into tragedy when Romeo Montague and Juliet Capulet fall in love. They marry in secret, but their happiness is short-lived as fate catches up with them. Mercutio's death and Tybalt's revenge lead to a final confrontation that ultimately ends the star-crossed lovers' lives together.
