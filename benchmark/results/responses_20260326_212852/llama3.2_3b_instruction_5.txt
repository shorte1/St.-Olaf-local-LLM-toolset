Prompt: Write a formal email declining a job offer, then rewrite it as a casual text message, then rewrite it as a Shakespearean monologue.
========================================
**Formal Email:**

Subject: Declining Job Offer for [Position]

Dear [Hiring Manager's Name],

I am writing to express my gratitude for the job offer extended to me for the position of [Position] at [Company Name]. I appreciate the time and effort taken by you and your team during the interview process.

After careful consideration, I regret to inform you that I have decided to decline the offer. Although I was impressed by the company culture and the role's responsibilities, I have decided to pursue another opportunity that aligns more closely with my long-term career goals.

Please accept my sincerest apologies for any inconvenience this may cause, and I wish the company continued success in finding a suitable candidate.

Thank you again for the opportunity, and I wish you all the best.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]

**Casual Text Message:**

"Hey [Hiring Manager's Name], it's [Your Name]. I wanted to thank you for the job offer, but I've decided to pass on it. Another opportunity came up that's a better fit for me. Sorry to miss out on the chance to work with you guys, but I appreciate the time you took to talk to me about the role. Good luck finding someone else!"

**Shakespearean Monologue:**

(O, wouldst thou hear my tale of woe?)

"O, fair [Hiring Manager's Name], how doth thy gentle hand offer unto me a position most divine?
A life of service and toil, 'twas promised to mine eager heart.
Yet, alas, I find myself in want of another dream,
One that doth stir within my breast a fire that doth not cease.

"Fie on this new opportunity, how it doth beckon me
To leave thy noble halls and join its radiant company!
For though thou hast shown me kindness and thy willingness to partake
In sharing with me the burdens of thy esteemed role,
I find myself ensnared in another's net of fortune's whims.

"Forgive my rejection, kind sir or madam, for I know
Thy heart doth ache at losing a candidate so fair and bright.
Yet still, I must decline thy offer and bid thee adieu.
May Fortune smile upon thee and guide thee to another worthy knight."

(Exeunt)
