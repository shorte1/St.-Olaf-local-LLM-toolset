Prompt: You have two ropes that each take exactly one hour to burn, but they burn unevenly. How do you measure exactly 45 minutes using only these ropes and a lighter?
========================================
Here's how you can measure exactly 45 minutes using the two ropes:

1. Light both ends of one rope.
2. When one end of the rope has burnt for 30 minutes, light the other unburnt portion with the same unlit portion.
3. At that point, about 15 minutes worth of rope is burned at each end (and 15 left in the middle).
