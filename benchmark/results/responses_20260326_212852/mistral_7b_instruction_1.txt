Prompt: Write a haiku about autumn, then translate it to French, then explain the seasonal symbolism used.
========================================
 Original Haiku:

Crimson leaves fall,
Whispering tales of time and change,
Autumn's symphony.

French Translation:

Feuilles rouges tombent,
Raconteant des histoires de temps et de changement,
Symphonie d'automne.

Seasonal Symbolism:

Crimson leaves (or red) symbolize the changing colors of autumn, which are often associated with the passage of time and the end of summer. The act of leaves falling from trees represents the gradual decline of nature during this season. The "tales of time and change" suggest the wisdom that comes with experiencing the seasons and acknowledging the cycle of life. Autumn's symphony symbolizes the beauty and harmony found in the changing seasons, as well as the melancholy feelings that often accompany them.
