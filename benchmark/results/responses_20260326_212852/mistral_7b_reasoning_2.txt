Prompt: If it takes 5 machines 5 minutes to make 5 widgets, how long would it take 100 machines to make 100 widgets? Show your working.
========================================
 Since each machine produces one widget in 5 minutes, with 100 machines, they can produce 100 widgets in the same amount of time, which is 5 minutes. This is because each machine works independently and there's no need for them to coordinate or wait for each other. Therefore, 100 machines can make 100 widgets in 5 minutes.

Alternatively, if we consider a situation where the production rate remains constant regardless of the number of machines (which is not explicitly stated but often assumed in such problems), then doubling the number of machines would double the production rate. In this case, with 100 machines, they could produce 200 widgets in 5 minutes because each machine makes one widget per minute and there are now twice as many machines. However, since the question asks for 100 widgets, the answer is still 5 minutes.
