Prompt: List the planets in our solar system by size from largest to smallest, give their diameter in km, and mark which ones have rings.
========================================
Certainly! Here is the list of the eight planets in our solar system arranged from the largest to the smallest based on their diameters:

1. **Jupiter** - Diameter: 139,820 km (Has rings)
2. **Saturn** - Diameter: 116,460 km (Has rings)
3. **Uranus** - Diameter: 50,724 km (Has rings)
4. **Neptune** - Diameter: 49,244 km
5. **Earth** - Diameter: 12,742 km
6. **Mars** - Diameter: 6,773 km
7. **Venus** - Diameter: 12,104 km
8. **Mercury** - Diameter: 4,879 km

Note that while all the giant planets (Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune) have rings to some extent, only Jupiter and Saturn are widely known for their prominent ring systems. Uranus and Neptune also possess rings but they are much fainter and less extensive compared to those of Jupiter and Saturn.
