========================================
Romeo and Juliet, from feuding families, secretly marry. Tragedy ensues: Tybalt kills Mercutio, Romeo retaliates; Juliet fakes death; Romeo, bereft, suicides; she awakens, unable to live without him, and dies, leading to tragic resolution.
