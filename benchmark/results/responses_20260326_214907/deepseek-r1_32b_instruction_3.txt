========================================
Romeo (Montague) and Juliet (Capulet) fall in love despite their families' feud. They secretly marry. Tybalt kills Mercutio; Romeo kills Tybalt and is exiled. Juliet fakes death to avoid marrying Paris, but Romeo, hearing of her 'death,' buys poison. He dies at her tomb; Juliet wakes and finds him dead, so she stabs herself.
