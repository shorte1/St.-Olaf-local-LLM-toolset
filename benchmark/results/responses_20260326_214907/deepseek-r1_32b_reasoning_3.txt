Prompt: A lily pad doubles in size every day. It takes 48 days to cover a lake. How long does it take to cover half the lake? Show your working.
========================================
To determine how long it takes for a lily pad that doubles its size every day to cover half of a lake, we can follow these steps:

1. **Understand the Growth Pattern:**
   - The lily pad doubles in size each day.
   - This means that if on day \( N \) it covers an area \( A \), on day \( N+1 \) it will cover \( 2A \).

2. **Given Information:**
   - It takes **48 days** for the lily pad to completely cover the lake.

3. **Determine When Half the Lake is Covered:**
   - Since the lily pad doubles in size every day, the area covered on day \( N \) is half of what it covers on day \( N+1 \).
   - Therefore, if the entire lake is covered on day 48, then on day **47**, the lily pad must have covered half the lake.
