**Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe**

**Prep Time:** 5 minutes  
**Cook Time:** 10-12 minutes  

---

### **Ingredients Section**

- **Dry Ingredients:**
  - 135g all-purpose flour
  - 75g sugar (caster or granulated)
  - 60g unsalted butter, cold and cut into cubes
  - 2 tsp baking soda
  - 1 tsp salt

- **Wet Ingredients:**
  - 4 large eggs, separated
  - 80ml milk (room temperature)
  - 3 tbsp chocolate chips, chopped (about 50g)

---

### **Steps Section**

1. **Preheat the Oven:** Preheat your oven to 190°C (375°F). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or line it with aluminum foil if using non-stick sheets.

2. **Combine Dry Ingredients:** In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, butter, baking soda, and salt until well combined.

3. **Add Wet Ingredients:** In a separate bowl, beat the eggs one at a time while incorporating the milk. Gradually stir in the melted chocolate chips.

4. **Mix Wet and Dry Ingredients:** Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

5. **Fold in Chocolate Chips:** Gently fold in the chopped chocolate chips until evenly distributed.

6. **Drop Cookie Dough Balls:** Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2-3 cm apart.

7. **Bake:** Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are set and the centers are still wobbly.

8. **Cool:** Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

---

Enjoy your batch of delicious chocolate chip cookies!
