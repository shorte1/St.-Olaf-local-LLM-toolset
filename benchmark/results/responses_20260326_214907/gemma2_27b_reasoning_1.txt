Prompt: A bat and a ball cost $1.10 in total. The bat costs $1.00 more than the ball. How much does the ball cost? Show your working.
========================================
Here's how to solve this classic riddle:

**Let:**

*  x = the cost of the ball

**Since the bat costs $1.00 more than the ball, we know:**

* The bat costs x + $1.00

**The total cost is $1.10, so:**

* x + (x + $1.00) = $1.10

**Now we can solve for x:**

1. Combine like terms: 2x + $1.00 = $1.10
2. Subtract $1.00 from both sides: 2x = $0.10
3. Divide both sides by 2: x = $0.05


**Therefore, the ball costs $0.05 (or 5 cents).**
