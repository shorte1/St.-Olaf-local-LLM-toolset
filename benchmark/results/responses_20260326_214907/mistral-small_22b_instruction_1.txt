Prompt: Write a haiku about autumn, then translate it to French, then explain the seasonal symbolism used.
========================================
 **Haiku in English:**

Leaves whisper secrets,
Crimson and gold dance with wind,
Autumn's breath embraces.

**Translation to French:**

Les feuilles murmurent des secrets,
Dansent avec le vent en rouge et or,
L'automne enlace par son souffle.

**Explanation of Seasonal Symbolism:**

1. **Leaves Whispering Secrets**: Autumn is a time of change and revelations. The leaves whisper secrets to signify the end of summer's vibrancy and the onset of autumn's introspection. This symbolizes the quiet reflection that often comes with the season.

2. **Crimson and Gold Dance with Wind**: The vivid colors of red (crimson) and gold are hallmarks of fall foliage. Their dance with the wind represents the fleeting beauty and transience of autumn. It also suggests the lively, dynamic nature of this season.

3. **Autumn's Breath Embraces**: Autumn is often associated with a gentle, cooling breeze that signals the end of summer heat. The embrace symbolizes the nurturing yet sometimes melancholic aspect of the season, where life prepares for dormancy and renewal.
