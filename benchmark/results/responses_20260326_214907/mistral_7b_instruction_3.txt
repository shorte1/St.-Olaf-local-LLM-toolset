 "Romeo & Juliet" narrates the tragic love story between two young lovers from feuding families in Verona. Against their families' wishes, they secretly marry, but a series of misunderstandings leads to a chain of events culminating in their tragic deaths, reuniting them as Romeo and Juliet awaken together in death.
