========================================
 Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

**Prep Time:** 15 minutes
**Cook Time:** 10-12 minutes

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
- 3/4 cup granulated sugar
- 3/4 cup packed light-brown sugar
- 2 large eggs
- 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 1/2 cups semisweet or milk chocolate chips (or a combination)
- 1 3/4 cups old-fashioned rolled oats (optional)

**Steps:**
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
2. Beat butter and sugars together in a large mixing bowl until light and creamy, about 2 minutes. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in vanilla extract.
3. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt; gradually add to butter mixture, mixing just until combined.
4. Fold in chocolate chips and oats (if using).
5. Drop dough by rounded tablespoonfuls onto prepared baking sheets, spacing about 2 inches apart.
6. Bake until cookies are golden around the edges but still soft in center, rotating sheets halfway through, about 10 to 12 minutes. Let cool on sheets on wire racks for 5 minutes; transfer cookies to wire racks and let cool completely.
7. Enjoy your homemade chocolate chip cookies!
