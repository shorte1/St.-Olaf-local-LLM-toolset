Prompt: If it takes 5 machines 5 minutes to make 5 widgets, how long would it take 100 machines to make 100 widgets? Show your working.
========================================
 Here's the logic behind this:

1. We know that 5 machines can produce 5 widgets in 5 minutes. This means that one machine produces one widget in 5 minutes (since there are 5 widgets and 5 machines).

2. Therefore, if we have 100 machines working simultaneously, each machine still takes 5 minutes to produce one widget.

3. Since all the machines are producing widgets at the same time, after 5 minutes, each of the 100 machines would have produced one widget.

4. So, it would take 5 minutes for 100 machines to make 100 widgets because each machine produces one widget in 5 minutes and there are 100 machines.

Hence, 100 machines can make 100 widgets in 5 minutes.
