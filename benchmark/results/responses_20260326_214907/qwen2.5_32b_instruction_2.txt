========================================
The planets of our solar system listed by size (diameter) from largest to smallest, along with whether they have rings:

1. **Jupiter** - Diameter: 139,820 km - Has Rings.
2. **Saturn** - Diameter: 116,460 km - Has Rings.
3. **Uranus** - Diameter: 50,724 km - Has Rings.
4. **Neptune** - Diameter: 49,244 km - Has Rings.
5. **Earth** - Diameter: 12,742 km - No rings.
6. **Venus** - Diameter: 12,104 km - No rings.
7. **Mars** - Diameter: 6,779 km - No rings.
8. **Mercury** - Diameter: 4,879 km - No rings.

Note that while Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune have ring systems, the most notable are those of Saturn due to their brightness and extension.
