Prompt: If it takes 5 machines 5 minutes to make 5 widgets, how long would it take 100 machines to make 100 widgets? Show your working.
========================================
To solve this problem, let's break down the information and work through it step by step:

### Step 1: Understand the Given Information
- We are told that 5 machines can produce 5 widgets in 5 minutes.

This implies:
- Each machine is producing 1 widget per 5 minutes (since 5 machines together make 5 widgets, each one must be making 1 widget).

### Step 2: Determine Production Rate per Machine
From the information above:
- One machine makes 1 widget in 5 minutes.
- Therefore, 100 machines would each also produce 1 widget in 5 minutes.

### Step 3: Apply This to 100 Machines and 100 Widgets
- If we have 100 machines all working simultaneously:
    - Each of these 100 machines can make 1 widget in 5 minutes.
    - Hence, in 5 minutes, the total output from 100 machines would be 100 widgets.

### Conclusion
Therefore, if you have 100 machines working at the same rate:
- It will take **5 minutes** for these 100 machines to produce 100 widgets.
