Prompt: Write a haiku about autumn, then translate it to French, then explain the seasonal symbolism used.
========================================
Haiku:
Leaves whisper down,
Crisp air whispers through branches,
Autumn's soft embrace.

Traduction en français :
Feuilles murmurent en descendant,
Air froid murmure parmi les branches,
La douce étreinte de l'automne.

Explanation of seasonal symbolism:
The haiku captures the essence of autumn with imagery that reflects both the sensory experience and emotional ambiance of the season. 

"Leaves whisper down" symbolizes the gentle transition of summer's green foliage into fall's colorful array, as leaves slowly detach from trees to carpet the ground. The use of "whisper" evokes a quiet, almost magical quality characteristic of autumn.

"Crisp air whispers through branches" highlights the change in temperature and humidity that accompanies falling temperatures. This line also symbolizes the shifting dynamics between nature and humanity as the weather turns cooler and people prepare for winter. 

"Autumn's soft embrace" personifies the season itself, offering a comforting but transient sensation of warmth before winter sets in. It encapsulates both the beauty and melancholy often associated with autumn—a time of harvest, reflection, and change.

Overall, this haiku uses natural elements to convey themes of transition, introspection, and the cycle of seasons, making it resonate deeply with many who experience autumn's unique qualities.
