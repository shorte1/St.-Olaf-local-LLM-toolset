Prompt: You have two ropes that each take exactly one hour to burn, but they burn unevenly. How do you measure exactly 45 minutes using only these ropes and a lighter?
========================================
To measure exactly 45 minutes using the two ropes that each take exactly one hour to burn (but not necessarily at a constant rate), follow these steps:

1. **Light both ends of the first rope and one end of the second rope simultaneously**.
2. The first rope, being lit from both ends, will burn out in 30 minutes because it effectively halves its burning time.
3. As soon as the first rope burns out completely after 30 minutes, light the other end of the second rope.
4. Since the second rope has been burning for 30 minutes from one end and now is also lit from the other end, it will take only 15 more minutes to burn completely.

By combining these steps, you effectively measure a total of 30 minutes (from step 2) plus an additional 15 minutes (from step 4), which adds up to exactly 45 minutes.
