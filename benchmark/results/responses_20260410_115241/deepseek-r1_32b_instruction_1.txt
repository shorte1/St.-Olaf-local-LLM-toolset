Prompt: Write a haiku about autumn, then translate it to French, then explain the seasonal symbolism used.
========================================
**Haiku in English:**

Leaves whisper as they fall,  
Colors blend in the crisp air,  
Trees shed their skin.  

**Translation to French:**

Feuilles chuchotent en tombant,  
Couleurs se mêlent dans l'air vif,  
Les arbres perdent leur peau.  

**Explanation of Seasonal Symbolism:**

- **Whispering Leaves:** The leaves whispering as they fall symbolize the gentle rustling sound and movement associated with autumn, evoking a sense of change and transition.
- **Blending Colors:** This line captures the vibrant yet harmonious mix of colors in autumn foliage, representing the beauty and fleeting nature of the season.
- **Shedding Skin:** Trees shedding their skin metaphorically represents renewal and readiness for winter, highlighting the cyclical process of growth and dormancy in nature.

Each element in the haiku reflects key aspects of autumn, blending sensory experiences with symbolic meanings to convey the essence of the season.
