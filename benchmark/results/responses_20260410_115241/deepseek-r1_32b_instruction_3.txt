========================================
In Verona, Romeo Montague and Juliet Capulet fall in love after meeting at a masquerade ball amidst their families' feuds. They secretly marry with Friar Laurence's help. Tybalt kills Mercutio; Romeo exiled for killing Tybalt. Juliet fakes death to avoid marriage to Paris but finds Romeo dead at the tomb, leading both to die by suicide and reconcile their families.
