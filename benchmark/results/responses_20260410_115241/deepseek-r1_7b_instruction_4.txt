========================================
**Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe**

---

### **Ingredients**
- 2 cups all-purpose flour  
- 1 cup granulated sugar  
- 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened  
- 3/4 cup brown sugar, packed  
- 2 large eggs  
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract  
- 1 teaspoon baking soda  
- 1/2 teaspoon salt  
- 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips  
- Optional: 1/2 cup cream cheese, softened (for glazing)  

---

### **Steps**
1. Preheat the oven to **375°F (190°C)**. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or line it with two sheets of parchment paper if making a double batch.  
2. In a large bowl, combine **1 cup of the flour** and the sugar. Add the softened butter and cut in with an electric mixer on medium speed until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.  
3. Add the brown sugar, eggs, vanilla extract, baking soda, and salt to the dry ingredients and mix just until combined. The dough should be slightly lumpy but not sticky.  
4. Stir in **2/3 of the chocolate chips** by hand or with a spatula. Sprinkle the remaining 1/3 of chocolate chips on top of the dough (or press them into the dough). Wrap the mixture tightly and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or until firm.  
5. Roll the dough between two sheets of parchment paper to transfer it to another sheet, then cut into small balls about **1 1/2 inches** in diameter. Place the dough balls on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper (or two sheets if making a double batch).  
6. Bake for **9-11 minutes**, or until the edges are golden brown but the centers are still soft. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for about 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

---

### **_prep Time:** 5 minutes  
### **Cook Time:** 9-11 minutes per batch
