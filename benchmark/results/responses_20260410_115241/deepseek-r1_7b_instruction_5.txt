O my poor mind, O heavy heart,  
For news of employment and such

But fate decreed I must decline.  
For in this hour of trial,  
My soul doth yearn for peace.  

I cannot stay to see the rise,  
Nor bear the weight of loss so heavy.  
For in this trying time,  
My spirit doth take flight,  
To seek a path new and bright.

And this rejection do I embrace.

For in this moment’s end,  
The will to soar shall never bend.
