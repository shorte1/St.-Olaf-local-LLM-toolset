## Autumn Haiku

Crimson leaves descend,
Whispering on cool breezes,
Winter slumber nears.


## French Translation

Feuilles rouges tombent,
Soufflées par un vent frais,
L'hiver approche.



## Seasonal Symbolism

This haiku utilizes several common autumnal symbols:

* **Crimson leaves:** The vibrant red color of leaves turning is a quintessential sign of autumn. This signifies the transition and eventual death of nature before winter.
* **Descending leaves:** Falling leaves represent letting go, change, and the passage of time. 
* **Cool breezes:** Autumn air often brings a refreshing coolness after the heat of summer, foreshadowing the approaching winter chill.

Together these images paint a picture of autumn's bittersweet beauty - a season of decline but also preparation for renewal in spring.
