## Formal Email

Subject: Job Offer - [Your Name]


Dear [Hiring Manager name],

Thank you very much for offering me the position of [Job title] at [Company name]. I appreciate you taking the time to interview me and share more about this exciting opportunity.

After careful consideration, I have decided to decline the offer at this time. This was not an easy decision as I was impressed by [Company name]'s culture and the team I met during the interview process. However, I have accepted another position that is a better fit for my current career goals. 

I wish you all the best in finding a suitable candidate for the role.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]


## Casual Text Message

Hey [Hiring Manager name], thanks again for the offer! I really appreciate it.  Unfortunately, I'm going to have to pass this time. I accepted another job that's a better fit right now. Good luck with the search! 

## Shakespearean Monologue

Hark! A missive doth arrive, bearing tidings fair,
Of fortune's smile and opportunity rare.
A post most worthy, at [Company name] they say,
For which my humble talents they would sway.

Yet though gratitude swells within my breast,
And admiration for their noble quest,
My heart doth lean towards a path untrod,
Where different winds do blow, by fate bestowed.

Another stage awaits, with roles to play,
A tapestry of dreams I must obey.
Thus, with regret, this offer I decline,
Hoping they find a soul whose stars align. 

May fortune smile upon their future quest,
And grant them candidates both wise and blessed.
