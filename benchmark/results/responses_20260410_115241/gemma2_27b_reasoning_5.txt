3. **Light the Other End:** Immediately light the other end of rope #2.  It was already burning from one end, so now it's burning from both sides.
4. **15 More Minutes:** Rope #2 will now burn out in 15 minutes (half its remaining burn time).

**Total Time: 30 minutes + 15 minutes = 45 minutes**
