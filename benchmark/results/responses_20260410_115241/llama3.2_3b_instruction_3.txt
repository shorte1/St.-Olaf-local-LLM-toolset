========================================
In Verona, two families, Montagues and Capulets, feud over loyalty and honor. Romeo, a Montague, falls for Juliet, a Capulet's daughter, at a ball. They secretly marry, but their love is doomed by family rivalry and fate. Tragedy unfolds as they ultimately succumb to their families' hatred and demise.
