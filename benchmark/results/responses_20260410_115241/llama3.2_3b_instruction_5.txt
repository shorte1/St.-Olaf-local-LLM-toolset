**Formal Email**

Subject: Declining Job Offer for [Job Title]

Dear [Hiring Manager's Name],

I am writing to express my gratitude for the job offer extended to me for the position of [Job Title] at [Company Name]. I was truly impressed by the company culture and the team's enthusiasm during my interview process.

However, after careful consideration, I regret to inform you that I have decided to decline the offer. While the role aligns with my skills and interests, I have decided to pursue another opportunity that better suits my long-term career goals.

Please know that my decision is in no way a reflection on the company or the position itself. I am confident that [Company Name] will continue to thrive and find an exceptional candidate for this role.

Thank you again for the opportunity, and I wish the organization continued success in the future.

Best regards,
[Your Name]

**Casual Text Message**

"Hey [Hiring Manager's Name], it's me. Just wanted to let you know that I've decided to pass on the job offer for [Job Title] at [Company Name]. The role was really cool, but another opportunity just came up that feels like a better fit for me. Thanks again for the chance, and I hope everything works out!"
