### 3) What sorting algorithm is this?
The function implements the **Quicksort** sorting algorithm. Quicksort is a divide-and-conquer algorithm that works by selecting a 'pivot' element from the array and partitioning the other elements into two sub-arrays, according to whether they are less than or greater than the pivot. The sub-arrays are then recursively sorted.

Quicksort is efficient for large datasets and is often used in practice due to its average-case efficiency and simplicity of implementation.
