Prompt: Write a haiku about autumn, then translate it to French, then explain the seasonal symbolism used.
========================================
 ### Haiku
### Autumn Moon
Moonlit nights whisper
Autumn's breath in golden hues
Nature's gentle sigh

### Translation to French
### Lune d'Automne
Les nuits lunaires murmurent
Le souffle d'automne dans des teintes dorées
Le soupir doux de la nature

### Explanation of Seasonal Symbolism
1. **Moonlit Nights Whisper**: The moon is a symbol often used in haiku to represent the passage of time and seasons. In autumn, the moonlight is often associated with the serenity and calmness of the season as days become shorter and nights longer.

2. **Autumn's Breath**: Autumn is personified here as breathing life into the surroundings. This breath can be seen in the gentle breeze that carries fallen leaves, signifying the change from summer to autumn.

3. **Golden Hues**: The golden color represents the changing foliage of the trees during fall. Leaves turning gold are a quintessential symbol of autumn, reflecting the season's warmth and natural beauty before the onset of winter.

4. **Nature's Gentle Sigh**: This symbolizes the peaceful and tranquil aspect of autumn. The sigh can be interpreted as nature taking a breath before the dormancy of winter, or the rustling of leaves underfoot as one walks through the forest, highlighting the season's ephemeral beauty.
