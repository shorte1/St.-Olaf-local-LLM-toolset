Prompt: A bat and a ball cost $1.10 in total. The bat costs $1.00 more than the ball. How much does the ball cost? Show your working.
========================================
 Let's denote the cost of the ball as x dollars.

The cost of the bat is then (x + 1) dollars.

According to the problem, the total cost of the bat and the ball is $1.10:

x (ball cost) + (x + 1) (bat cost) = 1.10

Combining the terms gives us:

x + x + 1 = 1.10

Simplifying this equation, we get:

2x + 1 = 1.10

Subtracting 1 from both sides:

2x = 0.10

Dividing by 2:

x = 0.05

So, the ball costs $0.05.
