Prompt: If it takes 5 machines 5 minutes to make 5 widgets, how long would it take 100 machines to make 100 widgets? Show your working.
========================================
 Since each machine makes one widget in 5 minutes, if we have 100 machines working simultaneously, they can all complete their widget at the same time after 5 minutes.

So, for 100 machines to make 100 widgets, it would still take 5 minutes. This is because each machine's production process doesn't depend on other machines and they all work independently.
