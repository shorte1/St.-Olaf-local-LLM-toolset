Questions: 1) What molecule do mitochondria produce? 2) Name two functions of mitochondria besides energy production. 3) What types of cells contain mitochondria?
========================================
1) Mitochondria primarily produce adenosine triphosphate (ATP), which is used as a source of chemical energy in the cell.
   
2) Besides energy production, mitochondria are involved in signaling and can influence cellular differentiation. They also play roles in managing cell death, maintaining control over the cell cycle, and regulating cell growth.

3) Mitochondria are found in eukaryotic cells. Eukaryotic cells are characterized by the presence of a nucleus and other membrane-bound organelles, which distinguish them from prokaryotic cells (such as bacteria).
