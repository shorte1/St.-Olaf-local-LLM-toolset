========================================
**Chocolate Chip Cookies**

*Prep Time:* 20 minutes  
*Cook Time:* 10-12 minutes

---

### Ingredients:
- 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
- 3/4 cup white sugar
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 2 large eggs
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 2 cups (about 12 oz) chocolate chips

### Steps:
1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper or lightly grease them.
2. In a small bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
3. Using an electric mixer in a large bowl, beat the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until creamy and smooth.
4. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in vanilla extract.
5. Gradually mix in the dry ingredients into the wet mixture just until combined.
6. Fold in chocolate chips with a spatula or wooden spoon.
7. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto prepared baking sheets.
8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until edges are lightly golden but centers still look soft.
9. Allow cookies to cool on the baking sheet for about 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Enjoy your freshly baked chocolate chip cookies!
