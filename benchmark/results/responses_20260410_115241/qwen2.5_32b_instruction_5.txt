**Formal Email Declining a Job Offer**

Subject: Rejection of Employment Offer

Dear [Employer's Name],

I am writing to express my gratitude for your kind offer of employment at [Company Name]. After careful consideration, I have decided not to accept this opportunity.

My decision is based on the need to pursue other career directions that align more closely with my long-term goals. While I greatly admire the work being done at [Company Name] and am honored by your confidence in me, I believe it would be best for both parties if we explore different paths.

Thank you once again for considering me for this position. Should an opportunity arise in the future that better aligns with my career aspirations, I hope to remain in contact regarding potential collaboration.

Best regards,

[Your Name]
