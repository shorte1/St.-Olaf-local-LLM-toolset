========================================

1. **Understanding the Doubling Process**: The lily pad doubles in size every day.
2. **Final Coverage**: On the 48th day, the lily pad covers the entire lake.
3. **Previous Day's Coverage**: Since the lily pad doubles in size each day, on the previous day (the 47th day), it would have covered exactly half of the lake.

Let's break this down step-by-step:

- On Day 48: The lily pad covers the entire lake.
- Therefore, on Day 47: The lily pad must have covered half of the lake because doubling its size from Day 47 to Day 48 would fill the whole lake.

So, it takes **47 days** for the lily pad to cover half of the lake.
