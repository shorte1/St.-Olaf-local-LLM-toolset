Prompt: You have two ropes that each take exactly one hour to burn, but they burn unevenly. How do you measure exactly 45 minutes using only these ropes and a lighter?
========================================
To measure exactly 45 minutes using the two ropes, follow these steps:

1. **Light both ends of the first rope and one end of the second rope simultaneously** with the lighter.
2. The first rope, being lit at both ends, will burn out in 30 minutes because it is burning twice as fast. 
3. When the first rope burns completely (after 30 minutes), light the other end of the second rope.
4. Since the second rope has been burning for 30 minutes from one end and now has its other end lit, it will take an additional 15 minutes to burn completely.

By following these steps, you effectively measure a total of 30 + 15 = 45 minutes.
